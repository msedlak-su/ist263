Welcome to my portfolio!
I'm Madeline Sedlak, a Syracuse University 2024 student graduating with a BFA in Fashion Design. I am also a multimedia artist who enjoys fashion design & styling, photography, and illustrating. Through each medium, I strive to evoke emotion, spark imagination, and inspire those around me. As a fashion designer, I channel my love for texture, color, and form into garments that tell stories and celebrate individuality. Whether it's draping fabrics or styling my friends, I find joy in the process of bringing my ideas to life. My photography consists of work from portraits of friends and family members, to snapshots of my travels and daily life. My drawings range from quick sketches to intricate illustrations, where I enjoy using several mediums to create a visual story. Join me on this journey of exploration and expression as we navigate the intersection of fashion, photography, and drawing. Thank you for joining me on my artistic journey!
